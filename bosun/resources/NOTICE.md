# Icon source

`uss_wisconsin_bb64_crest.png` is cropped (transparent background) from the
official U.S. Navy coat of arms for USS Wisconsin (BB-64).

Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:USS_Wisconsin_(BB-64)_coat_of_arms.jpg

License: Public domain (work of a U.S. Navy employee in an official
capacity — a work of the U.S. federal government).

`uss_wisconsin_bb64_crest_tray.png` is a lightened derivative (dark tones
blended toward white) used for the system tray icon, for better visibility
on dark panels/taskbars at small sizes.
