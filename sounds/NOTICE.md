# Real audio sources

These files are optional, real (non-synthesized) recordings used by the
"Real Recordings" audio-source mode. If a file is missing, Bosun falls back
to its synthesized tone automatically.

## whistle.wav — bosun's pipe

Source: "Piping the Side," from *Boatswain's Calls*, performed by the
United States Navy Band Ceremonial Band.
https://archive.org/details/BoatswainsCalls

License: Public Domain Mark 1.0 (U.S. federal government work).

Converted from the original MP3 to mono 44.1kHz WAV; no other changes.

## bell_2.wav .. bell_8.wav — ship's bell strikes

Source: "Two Bells, Ship Time.wav" through "Eight Bells, Ship Time.wav" by
freesound.org user Benboncan.
https://freesound.org/people/Benboncan/

License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0).
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Converted from the freesound preview MP3 to mono 44.1kHz WAV; no other
changes. Attribution: "Ship Time bell recordings by Benboncan (freesound.org),
CC BY 4.0."

No "one bell" recording under a compatible license was available, so a
single-bell strike always uses the synthesized tone even in Real Recordings
mode.
